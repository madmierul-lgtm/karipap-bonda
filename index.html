Our Story

A Recipe Passed Down
Through Generations

Karipap Bonda began in 1998 when Mak Bonda first started selling her legendary curry puffs at the Pasar Minggu in Kuala Lumpur. Her secret? A perfectly flaky pastry, a curry filling that simmers for hours, and an unmistakable touch of love in every fold.

Today, we've grown from a small market stall to a beloved household name — but our recipe and our heart remain exactly the same. Every karipap is still hand-crimped and fried fresh to order.
